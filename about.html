/ The Artist (01)

Michael Adkins is a professional saxophonist holding a Doctor of Musical Arts (DMA) degree. His career is defined by a "less is more" intentionality, rooted in the creative hubs of New York and Boston.

His work as both a performer and educator balances rigorous technical mastery with a deep commitment to the spontaneity of jazz improvisation.
